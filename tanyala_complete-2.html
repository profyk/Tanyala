Our Story

Why Tanyala Exists

The countries of Southern Africa share structural failures that have persisted across decades and different governments. These are not accidents. They are the consequences of political choices made by people who benefited from them.

Tanyala was born from a simple truth: the people who are suffering are not the problem. The system built to serve a few at the expense of the many is the problem. And systems can be changed.

We are not a protest movement. We are a governing project. We are building the structures, the leadership, and the citizen power to take this nation back — ward by ward, community by community, election by election.

Our Manifesto

What We Stand For

"We did not choose this anger. It was given to us by decades of broken promises, stolen resources, and governments that treated the poor as a problem to manage — not citizens to be served."

Our six policy pillars are specific, costed, and defensible. We make fewer, harder promises — and keep them.

Become a Member Fund Our Work
⚡

Economic Justice & Jobs

One million jobs in 3 years through infrastructure, green energy, and small business support. A living wage for every worker.

⚖

Zero Tolerance Corruption

An independent anti-corruption authority with prosecutorial powers. Public asset declarations. Transparent procurement.

📚

Education for the Real Economy

Universal early childhood education. Skills reform for trades, technology, and entrepreneurship. No student expelled for fees.

🏥

Healthcare Within Reach

No citizen more than 10km from basic medical care. Mental health and chronic disease treated as national emergencies.

🕳

Real Democracy

Electoral reform, strengthened parliamentary oversight, protected press freedom, and guaranteed right to protest.

🌿

Youth & Community Power

Young people are the present, not the future. Community-led development. 30% government procurement for youth enterprises.

No single donor may contribute more than 5% of our total annual budget. This prevents financial capture by any individual or corporation.
